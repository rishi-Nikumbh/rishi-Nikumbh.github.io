About Me

I'm a Mechanical and Clean Energy Engineer completing my M.Sc. in Clean Energy Technologies at Friedrich Alexander University Erlangen. My work sits at the intersection of electrochemical systems, hands-on engineering, and rigorous validation methodology.

I have practical experience designing and executing DVP&R-based test plans for fuel-cell and electrolysis components — from material qualification (tensile, shear, hardness, leak) to accelerated lifetime testing. I bridge R&D and production: translating functional requirements into process specs, tooling designs, and quality criteria.

Beyond testing, I design and build the hardware needed — custom fixtures, vacuum tooling, 3D-printed jigs, and test rigs — and I optimise production processes with measurable results.
